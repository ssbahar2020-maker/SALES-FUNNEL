Refund Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2025

Because our products are digital and delivered instantly upon purchase, all sales are generally final. Please read below for our full policy and exceptions.

All Sales Are Final — Digital Products

Due to the instant delivery nature of digital products, we do not offer refunds after a digital file has been downloaded or accessed. This is standard practice for digital goods and is consistent with consumer protection guidelines for digital downloads.

Before purchasing, we encourage you to:

  • Read the full product description and chapter list on our sales page
  • Review the FAQ section on our sales page
  • Contact us at 470-455-5515 with any questions before purchasing

Exceptions — When We Will Consider a Refund

We will consider a refund request in the following limited circumstances:

  • Technical failure: You were charged but did not receive a download link and our support team was unable to resolve the issue within 48 hours
  • Duplicate charge: You were charged more than once for the same order
  • Unauthorized charge: You can demonstrate that the purchase was made without your authorization

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund under the exceptions above, contact us within 7 days of your purchase date with:

  • Your full name and email address used at checkout
  • Order confirmation number
  • Description of the issue
